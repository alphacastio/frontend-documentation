# Apply transform

In this step, the user will be able to make changes to the original data depending on the frequency of the data and combine the different transformations. Some of them make it possible to change the data from current prices to constant prices, deseasonalize time series, change from local currency to dollars, or even express them as a percentage of GDP or per capita. Variations can also be made month over month, year over year, the sum of 12 months, variations a year ago, and many more

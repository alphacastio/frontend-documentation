# Publish Datasets

This step is one of the three outputs. This step it enables you to create a new dataset that's automatically updated from your pipelines.

![image.png](https://alphacast-images.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/next-s3-uploads/6b381b0b-2a1c-4230-adab-b65c1c4147e8/image.png)

Once you have made all the transformations to your data you will be able to publish a dataset as a result 

This step allows you to choose the name of the dataset and the repository where it will be stored

![image.png](https://alphacast-images.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/next-s3-uploads/fa32986c-034d-43aa-8684-66bdab4a8422/Publish-to-Dataset.jpg)

After you have named and saved your dataset, click the link to navigate the new dataset

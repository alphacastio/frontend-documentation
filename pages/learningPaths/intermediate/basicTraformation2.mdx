**Step by Step Guide: Change Frequency & Units**

**01.** *Fetch Yahoo Finance Ticker*

To create a pipeline by clicking on Create Pipeline at the top right corner. Select a name and a repository for your pipeline, then choose **Yahoo Finance Fetch**.

![Interm - part 2 - fetch posta.png](https://alphacast-images.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/next-s3-uploads/8c1e5fda-0ab8-411e-8a9e-4c319ac322d3/Interm---part-2---fetch-posta.png)

**02.** *Change Frequency*

Adding the **Change Frequency** step to select your daily data and convert it to either monthly, quarterly, or yearly data. In this example, we will select the **Close** column and we will change the frequency by selecting a formula for monthly data. 

![image](https://alphacast-images.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/next-s3-uploads/80a00e94-1c37-4be8-a8f9-d09e655c7a87/Interm---part-2---freq.jpg)

**03.** *Change Unit*

For this example, we will remove the effect of inflation on prices, you can change the unit by adding the **Apply Country Trasform** step.

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/106676128/221672781-f1344885-f97c-45dc-8df5-404b46d0e42b.png)

It will recognize the frequency of your dataset, and the available options will depend on it. The usual options include changing the unit to constant prices, percentage of GDP, dollars, or per capita.

![image](https://alphacast-images.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/next-s3-uploads/59b8b86d-7e20-46c8-bfbd-61a540c78988/interm---part-2---units-p.jpg)
